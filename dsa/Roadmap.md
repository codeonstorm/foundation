# Complete DSA Roadmap

This roadmap is designed to take you from basic programming fluency to interview-ready Data Structures and Algorithms. Follow it in order, but keep revising older topics while learning new ones.

## How To Use This Roadmap

- Pick one programming language and use it consistently.
- Learn the concept first, then implement it from scratch.
- Solve easy problems before moving to medium problems.
- Keep a notebook for patterns, mistakes, and reusable templates.
- Revisit solved problems after 3 days, 7 days, and 21 days.
- Do not memorize solutions. Learn the decision process.

## Recommended Weekly Structure

- 5 days: Learn topic plus solve problems.
- 1 day: Mixed revision from older topics.
- 1 day: Contest, mock interview, or timed practice.
